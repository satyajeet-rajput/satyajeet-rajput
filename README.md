## 🧬 About Me

I'm a **Senior Test Engineer** with **4+ years of experience** in software quality assurance and test automation. Based in **Pune, India 🇮🇳**, I specialize in building robust automation frameworks using **Playwright** and **Selenium**, and crafting comprehensive API test suites with **RestAssured**.

Over the course of my career at **Coforge**, I have delivered end-to-end quality assurance across enterprise-grade applications in the **Airline, Railway, and Travel** domains — working with globally recognized brands like **FINNAIR, AMADEUS, Eurostar, and TTC**.

I am passionate about leveraging **Generative AI and LLM-based techniques** to build smarter, self-healing test automation that reduces maintenance overhead and accelerates delivery. I thrive in **Agile/Scrum** environments, collaborating closely with developers, product managers, and stakeholders to ship high-quality software with confidence.
